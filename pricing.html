APR Wire Licensing Pricing

Access Tiers

All-Pro Reels Wire offers licensing structured around your publication's size, usage needs, and delivery speed requirements.

Single-Game License

One-off access to a specific game or event gallery. Ideal for one-time coverage needs.

Season Package

Ongoing access across a full season for a given team. Best for publications with regular beat coverage.

Wire Subscription

Standing access across our full DC sports coverage, delivered as we shoot. Our most comprehensive tier, built for publications that need continuous access.

What Determines Your Rate

  • Volume - how many images/galleries you need access to
  • Usage - editorial only, or broader commercial/print use
  • Delivery speed - in-game live feed, same-night post-game, or next-day archive access

Rates are custom-quoted based on these factors - reach out and we'll put together a package that fits your publication.
