About Us

APR Wire by All-Pro Reels

Mission

APR Wire is the syndication arm of All-Pro Reels, a credentialed sports and entertainment media company founded in 2019 in Washington, DC. Our roots go back further - to Hoop District, a DC sports media outlet co-founded in 2007 - but our mission has stayed the same: put the right photographer in the right building, every night, and get the image into an editor's hands before the story goes cold.

Service

All-Pro Reels holds credentialed press access to Washington's professional sports franchises and select national events, producing on-site coverage that has appeared in outlets including ESPN, The Washington Post, The Athletic, CBS Sports, and others. APR Wire packages that coverage for syndication - giving publications, blogs, and media outlets a direct line to licensed, deadline-ready photography without needing their own credentialed photographer in every building.

Additional Services

Teams, venues, sponsors, and event organizers can work with All-Pro Reels for dedicated event photography and branded content, including corporate headshots and on-site brand coverage.

Content Syndication

Have your own archive or ongoing coverage you'd like to distribute? Contact us to discuss syndication partnerships.
